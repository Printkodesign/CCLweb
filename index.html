About Us

Connect Community Living is dedicated to providing unparalled home-care support for families who need assistance in caring for their loved ones in their homes.

Our Board of Directors all began as Private Homeshare providers, taking pride in providing a high quality of in-home support, developed from years of experience encountering the challenges within the home care system. Connect Community Living was built to address such challenges by improving the quality of care while reducing the cost of our clients.

Our Mission

At Connect Community Living, our mission is to create a lasting impact by fostering strong connections with families and communities. We are dedicated to providing unparalleled service that goes beyond mere support, aiming to build a network of empowered individuals and thriving communities. We endeavor to break down barriers and cultivate understanding, acceptance, and support for all the individuals we serve. Through our unwavering commitment to inclusivity and collaboration, we strive to be a catalyst for positive change. Our mission is to understand the unique needs of every individual, family and community we serve, creating tailored solutions that empower individuals and strengthen the bonds that tie communities together. We aim to be a trusted partner in every stage of life, connecting with families to provide holistic support and collaborating with communities to address shared challenges. By promoting open communication, cultural sensitivity, and a deep understanding of diverse needs, we aspire to build a more resilient and interconnected society.

Our Vision

At Connect Community Living, our vision is to create a world where individuals with special needs not only receive exceptional support but also thrive as active, valued members of their communities. We envision a society where families are empowered, communities are inclusive, and diversity is celebrated. We aspire to be a beacon of innovation and compassion in the field of special needs services, pioneering approaches that not only address the unique challenges faced by individuals with special needs but also promote their strengths and abilities. Our vision extends beyond conventional support, aiming to create an environment where every individual, regardless of their abilities, can lead a life full of opportunities and meaningful connections. We see a future where families of individuals with special needs experience a sense of belonging, understanding, and continuous growth. Our commitment to collaboration with communities is at the core of our vision, striving to break down barriers and foster a culture of acceptance, diversity, and support. By envisioning a society where everyone is valued for their unique contributions, we aim to lead the way in building a community that recognizes and embraces the potential of every individual. At Connect Community Living, our vision is to create lasting positive change, leaving an indelible mark on the lives of individuals with special needs, their families, and the communities we serve."

Our Core Values

At Connect Community Living, our core values shape our identity and guide our actions, reflecting our unwavering commitment to excellence and positive impact. These values define who we are:

  • Commitment: We are deeply committed to the individuals and communities we serve. Our dedication is evident in our persistent pursuit of excellence and our promise to go above and beyond to meet the diverse needs of those we support.

  • Leadership: We lead with purpose and vision. Our commitment to leadership is not just about authority but about inspiring others, fostering innovation, and driving positive change both within our organization and in the broader community.

  • Passion: Passion fuels our work. We approach every task with enthusiasm, energy, and a genuine love for what we do. It is this passion that propels us to continuously seek creative solutions and make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

  • Excellent Service: Excellence is non-negotiable. We consistently deliver top-tier services, leveraging our passion and commitment to provide the highest quality support. Our pursuit of excellence is a testament to our dedication to positively impact individuals and communities.

  • Teamwork: Collaboration is our strength. We believe in the power of teamwork, recognizing that diverse perspectives and skills enhance our collective capabilities. Together, we achieve more, fostering an environment where every team member's contribution is valued.

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards. Integrity is the cornerstone of our relationships, built on transparency, honesty, and accountability. We earn trust through our actions, fostering a culture where integrity is non-negotiable.

  • Diversity: We celebrate diversity in all its forms. We believe that embracing differences enhances our creativity, innovation, and effectiveness. Inclusion is embedded in our culture, ensuring that every individual feels valued and heard.

  • Goal: We set and achieve ambitious goals. Our focus on clear objectives drives our actions, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and ensuring that we make a meaningful impact aligned with our mission.

  • Positivity: We cultivate a positive and uplifting environment. Positivity is not just a mindset; it's a guiding force that fuels resilience, fosters collaboration, and creates a culture where challenges are seen as opportunities for growth.

  • Statement of Purpose: At Connect Community Living,, we are unwavering in our commitment to put God at the forefront of all we do. Our core value is grounded in the belief that every action, decision, and service we provide should be a testament to our devotion to God. We seek to align our mission, vision, and daily operations with the principles and teachings that reflect our faith. By integrating God into every aspect of our work, we aim to bring honor and glory to His name.

At Connect Community Living, these core values are the compass that directs our actions, ensuring that we remain steadfast in our commitment to leadership, passion, excellent service, teamwork, integrity, diversity, and the pursuit of positive and impactful goals.

Services

Community Based Support

"Empowering Lives, Building Communities: Our Community Based Support Services are the cornerstone of positive change. At Connect Community Living, we are dedicated to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for individuals in need.

At Connect Community Living, we believe in the transformative power of connection. Join us as we reach out, uplift, and make a lasting impact. Together, let's build stronger, more resilient communities where everyone has the opportunity to thrive in their communities. Experience the difference with Connect Community Living, Community Inclusion and Outreach Services because every connection we make is a step toward a brighter, more inclusive future."

Homeshare and Shared Living

"Harmony in Shared Spaces: Welcome to Connect Community Living, where we redefine living through the magic of Homesharing. Our innovative Homeshare providers bring together individuals seeking companionship and shared living arrangements, fostering a sense of community and mutual support. With a focus on shared values, interests, and respect, we turn houses into homes and strangers into family. Join us in the Homeshare experience where every shared moment becomes a cherished memory, and every home becomes a place of connection, understanding, and shared joy."

Homemaker

"Elevate Your Lifestyle: At Connect Community Living, we redefine comfort and convenience with our personalized homemaker solutions. Our dedicated team of homemakers is committed to transforming your living space into a haven of tranquility, allowing you to savor life's precious moments without the stress of household chores. Our dedicated team of compassionate professionals is committed to providing personalized and dignified personal care solutions tailored to your unique needs. From grooming and hygiene assistance to specialized care plans, we prioritize your well-being, ensuring you feel empowered, confident, and cared for every day”.

Respite

"Rest, Renew, Relieve: Introducing Connect Community Living Respite Services, where care meets compassion to provide families with the vital support they need. Life's challenges can be overwhelming, and that's where we step in, offering a haven for both caregivers and their loved ones. Our Respite Services are designed to provide a temporary break, ensuring that families can recharge, knowing their loved ones are in capable hands. With a team of dedicated and trained professionals, we create a nurturing environment that focuses on individual needs, fostering a sense of well-being for all. Join us in embracing the power of respite because everyone deserves a moment to breathe, and every family deserves the care they can trust."

Staffed Residential

"Elevating Residential Living: Connect Community Living Staffed Residential Services takes pride in providing comfort, care, and community converge. Our expertly trained staff is dedicated to providing unparalleled support, creating a home where residents flourish. From personalized care plans to engaging activities, we prioritize the well-being and individuality of each resident. At Connect Community Living, we redefine residential living with a commitment to dignity, respect, and a vibrant sense of community. Join us in experiencing the next level of care and comfort – where every residence is more than just a place to stay; it's a thriving community that feels like home. Discover the difference with Connect Community Living Staffed Residential Services, where your well-being is our top priority."
